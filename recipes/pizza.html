Pizza

Description

This is a classic homemade pizza recipe, including a pizza dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step instructions with photos. Make perfect pizza at home!

Ingredients

  • 355 ml warm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 490 g bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat pizza stone (or pizza pan or baking sheet):
  5. Divide the dough into two balls
  6. Prep toppings
  7. Flatten dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  8. Brush dough top with olive oil
  9. Sprinkle pizza peel with corn meal, put flattened dough on top
  10. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  11. Sprinkle cornmeal on pizza stone, slide pizza onto pizza stone in oven
  12. Bake pizza
